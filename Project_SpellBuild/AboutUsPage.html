Feature of Spell Build
Classic Mode

play 5 vs 5 with other players all around the world without trophy.

Ranked Mode

Play with other players with rank and leaderboard.

Survival Mode

Travel around Spell Build world and defeat your enemy.


Basic Mechanic of Spell Build
Wind Jumping

Jumping aroung the wind by click [SHIFT] twice.

Gauntle Rotation

Rotate your hero by scroll your mouse or keypad.

Hero Skills

Every hero have 5 skills. Skills can be used by click A-G keyboard.
